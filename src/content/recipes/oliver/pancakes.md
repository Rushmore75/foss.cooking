## Ingredients

## Dry
- 1C. Flour
- 2T. Sugar
- 1t. Baking Powder
- 1/2t. Baking Soda
- 1/4t. Salt

## Wet
- 1C. Buttermilk (Normal milk works just fine)
- 1T. Oil (Olive Oil, Butter, Etc)
- 1 Egg, beaten

- Optionally; blueberries (as many as looks good)

## Directions

1. Combine all of your dry ingredients, mixing them together.
1. Add in all the wet ingredients, mixing them in.
    1. If adding blueberries, you may need to add more milk
1. Cook in a pan on medium heat, flip when the bubbles on the edge of the pancake don't close up after popping.
1. Enjoy!
